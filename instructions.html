How to Participate

Welcome to the Digits N Data Challenge! This is a 6-week journey where you solve SQL questions, unlock the next one, and showcase your creativity. Here’s how it works:

Step 1: Stay Updated

Follow Digits N Data, Nitish, and Muskan on LinkedIn. This ensures you don’t miss any updates or hints about the challenge.

Step 2: Solve the Questions

There will be 6 questions, one per week. Each question is like a puzzle—you solve it using SQL queries.

Step 3: Share Your Answers

Post your query (every week) on LinkedIn, tag us (Digits N Data, Nitish and Muskan) and use hashtags (#SixWeeksToSaveReality, #DNDchallenge, #DigitsnData). Only after submitting your answer will the next question unlock for you.

Step 4: Be Creative with Your Final Submission

After completing all 6 questions, you have 2 days to present your work creatively. You can make a video, presentation, or any creative format to show how you solved the queries. Post this on LinkedIn or YouTube and tag us. We’ll check it out and analyze your work.

Step 5: Show Your Creativity

This challenge isn’t just about getting the answers right. We want to see how you express your work, your style, and your creativity.

Quick Tips to Win

  • Solve each question carefully and ensure your queries are correct.
  • Be original and creative in your final presentation.
  • It’s okay to take help, but don’t copy—this is your time to learn, stand out and show your creativity.
  • Don’t forget to post your work on LinkedIn or YouTube with proper tags.

Important Notes

  1. Save your queries somewhere safe for the final task (we are not storing your queries on our end).
  2. The result you get in each question will act as your password for the next question. If you're stuck here, reach out to Muskan on Linkedin.
  3. If you believe your query is correct but the system marks it incorrect, don’t worry — there are multiple valid ways to approach SQL problems.

    In that case:
    • Save your result somewhere and use it as the password. If your query is correct, your result will unlock the question., or

    • Email us at digitsndata@gmail.com
  4. If you face any technical glitch on the website and can’t move ahead, reach out to Nitish, or email us at digitsndata@gmail.com.
