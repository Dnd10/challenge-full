Final Task Instructions — Your Creative Showcase

You’ve completed all six queries. Now it’s time for the part that truly sets you apart.

This final task isn’t just about SQL anymore. It’s about how you tell the story of what you did, what you discovered, and how you think. This is the moment where your personality, your creativity, and your voice matter.

Here’s what you need to do:

1. Collect Everything You’ve Done

Gather:

  • Each SQL question
  • The query you wrote
  • The answer you arrived at

This is your raw material.

2. Explain Your Thinking

For every query:

  • Explain what the question was actually asking
  • Describe how you approached it
  • Share why you chose that specific query
  • Highlight what the final answer was

Keep it simple, clear, and human. We're not searching for perfection — we're looking for how you think.

3. Present It Creatively

This is where you get to shine.

You can choose any format that reflects your style:

Options you can pick from:

  • A mini presentation (Google Slides, Canva, Notion — anything)
  • A short video where you speak through your process
  • A storytelling format (turn the simulation into characters, events, timelines… go wild)
  • A simple website or a fun interactive page
  • A narrated breakdown as if you're a detective solving a paradox
  • A comic-strip style explanation
  • A sci-fi themed debrief
  • Or something completely your own — surprise us

Your goal is not to be fancy.
Your goal is to be memorable.

Post it on Linkedin and tag us(Digits N Data, Nitish, and Muskan).

Most people will post robotic explanations or copy–paste queries.
That’s not what stands out.
Your creativity, your clarity, and your voice will.

4. Why This Matters

This task isn’t just for the reward from Digits N Data.

We genuinely want you to create something that could catch the eye of:

  • Recruiters
  • Collaborators
  • Founders
  • Other learning communities

People respond to originality, confidence, and personality far more than generic posts.
This is your chance to practice that.

5. Final Reminder

Show effort.
Show curiosity.
Show creativity.
Show you.
